  - [**Demo Pivot:** The demo must pivot from a hospital-specific product to a custom ERP solution for a gas company, positioning Adamrit as a proof-of-concept for our development capabilities.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3346.0)
  - [**Strategic Narrative:** The core message is that standard ERPs are rigid and expensive. ## Topics

### Demo Rehearsal & Feedback

  - [The initial Adamrit demo was too generic and lacked a clear narrative.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=4152.0)
  - [**Feedback:** The presentation must be structured around a logical user flow (e.g., patient registration → OPD/IPD → discharge) to be effective.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=1650.0)
  - [**Key Example:** The Slack integration for maintenance tickets at Tendaya Kerala hospital is a strong, tangible example of a real-world AI automation.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3485.0)

### Strategic Narrative: Custom ERP vs. Standard ERP

  - [**Problem:** Standard ERPs are rigid, expensive, and force clients into recurring subscriptions.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=4268.0)
  - [**Solution:** A custom-built, AI-powered "enterprise brain" that acts as a central memory layer, connecting and orchestrating a client's existing third-party tools.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3120.0)
  - [**Proof Point:** Adamrit's success in hospitals proves our ability to build complex, enterprise-grade systems for any industry.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3346.0)

### Technical & Execution Plan

  - [**Accounting:** Use DALI as the system of record, with Microvista for e-invoicing.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=2726.0)
  - [**Cloud:** OCI (Oracle Cloud Infrastructure) for both IaaS (compute, storage) and AI services.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=4013.0)
      - [**Rationale:** Using OCI's native AI services simplifies the tech stack and ensures data privacy compliance (e.g., GDPR).](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=4013.0)
  - [**Phased Migration:** A low-risk transition strategy.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3407.0)
    1.  [**Parallel Run:** The new custom ERP operates alongside the client's existing Odoo system.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3407.0)
    2.  [**Gradual Cutover:** Functionality is migrated from Odoo to the custom ERP in stages.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3407.0)
    3.  [**Retirement:** After a stable \~90-day parallel run, the Odoo system is retired.](https://fathom.video/share/eGqvLFnNDLw-sunyCCNHMMqu_SPyCBSv?tab=summary&timestamp=3407.0)
