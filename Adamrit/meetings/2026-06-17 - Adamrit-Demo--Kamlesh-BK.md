# Adamrit Demo- Kamlesh & BK

*2026-06-17 — [Open in Fathom](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK)*

## Meeting Purpose

[Demo the Adam Ritz hospital management system.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=236.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**Core Functionality:** The system handles patient registration, OPD/IPD management, billing, and pharmacy operations.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=241.0)
  - [**AI-Assisted Workflows:** AI generates suggestions for lab tests, radiology, and medications, streamlining order entry.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=603.0)
  - [**Internal Development:** Built and used daily in the presenter’s two hospitals, ensuring practical, real-world validation.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1294.0)
  - [**Key Gaps:** Lacks a PACS for imaging data and a barcode system for pharmacy inventory, which are planned for future development.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1108.0)

## Topics

### Patient Management & Billing

  - [**Registration:** Captures demographics, impanelment status (e.g., PMJY), and visit type (OPD/IPD).](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=262.0)
  - [**Visit Conversion:** A single click converts an OPD visit to IPD, auto-assigning available wards and beds.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=482.0)
  - [**Billing:**](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=570.0)
      - [Add diagnoses and surgeries (e.g., Laminectomy).](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=570.0)
      - [AI-generated suggestions for lab tests, radiology, and medications.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=603.0)
      - [Capture surgery details: operating doctor, anesthetist, implants.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=620.0)
      - [Admin dashboard tracks admissions and bill aging; admin approval is required for all bills.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1128.0)

### Pharmacy & Inventory

  - [**Pharmacy Module:** Manages direct sales, stock, and discount approvals.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=855.0)
  - [**Inventory Gap:** Lacks a barcode/QR system for stock management.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1247.0)

### Reporting & Admin

  - [**Discharge Summaries:** AI-assisted generation pulls data from the patient's record (diagnoses, treatments, surgeries) to create a comprehensive summary.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=660.0)
  - [**Gate Pass:** A required document printed for patients upon discharge.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1038.0)
  - [**Document Management:** Uploads patient documents (referral data, ESIC card) directly to their file.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=995.0)

### System Context

  - [**Development Status:** Built and used internally in the presenter’s two hospitals.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1294.0)
  - [**PACS Gap:** The system currently only handles CT/MRI *descriptions*, not the imaging data itself. A full PACS is planned for future development.](https://fathom.video/share/xBYwxw3UpGnN_zxEwxz2oPYzzFrxVjUK?tab=summary&timestamp=1108.0)
