# BNI 121 MEETING

*2026-06-08 — [Open in Fathom](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2)*

## Meeting Purpose

[To explore business synergies between a hospital and a pharma distributor.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=17.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**Distributor's Value Prop:** A third-gen pharma distributor (1,200 wholesale clients, 600 doctors) offers hospitals a unique model: they buy excess stock and handle billing, allowing hospitals to meet supplier minimums without inventory risk.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=211.0)
  - [**Key Hospital Needs:** Dr. Murali's Nagpur hospitals (Hope, Raishman) have high consumption of surgicals, disposables, injectables (antibiotics), and cardiology drugs.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=902.0)
  - [**Strategic Advice:** The distributor recommended a central trading firm for Dr. Murali to streamline purchasing, consolidate GST, and simplify inter-hospital transfers.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=480.0)
  - [**Action Plan:** Dr. Murali will connect his purchasing manager with the distributor to explore immediate trading opportunities, starting with a review of current purchase orders.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=1140.0)

### Distributor's Value Proposition

  - [**Profile:** Third-generation pharma distributor with a large client base (1,200 wholesale, 600 doctors).](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=211.0)
  - [**Core Service → Inventory Risk Reduction:**](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=211.0)
      - [**Problem:** Hospitals must meet supplier minimums (e.g., 200 strips of a drug) but only need a fraction (e.g., 40).](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
      - [**Solution:** The distributor buys the excess stock (160 strips) and handles billing, enabling the hospital to meet the minimum without holding unnecessary inventory.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=275.0)
  - [**Product Liquidation:** Buys branded products from hospitals at a 3% margin to help clear excess stock.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=420.0)
  - [**Distribution Portfolio (Nasik District):**](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=361.0)
      - [Flamingo (8-year exclusive)](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=361.0)
      - [Amruth Noni (Ayurvedic)](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=361.0)
      - [Sell Nutra (Protein powders)](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=361.0)
  - [**Retail Channel:**](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=961.0)
      - [12 doctors prescribe to their retail outlets.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=961.0)
      - [Offers 15% discount and home delivery.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=961.0)
      - [Serves Sanjini Homeopathy (22 branches) with B12 injections.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=1020.0)

### Strategic Recommendation: Central Trading Firm

  - [**Rationale:** A central firm would streamline purchasing, consolidate GST, and simplify inter-hospital transfers, avoiding complex billing between the two hospitals.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=480.0)
  - [**Implementation:** A simple firm (₹800/year) operating from a laptop and a small (\~200 sq ft) space.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=480.0)
  - [**Recommended Software:** Tally Prime (₹25k/year) for integrated accounting, billing, and GST.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=601.0)

### Distributor's Operational Model

  - [**Payment Terms:** Weekly market clearance on Saturdays to prevent cash from being tied up in receivables.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=780.0)
  - [**Stock Management:** Holds inventory for a maximum of 15 days to minimize damage, expiry, and capital lockup.](https://fathom.video/share/cEz3X5n5-zMc-ZY4nyZ5myHcEYxzDjb2?tab=summary&timestamp=1080.0)
