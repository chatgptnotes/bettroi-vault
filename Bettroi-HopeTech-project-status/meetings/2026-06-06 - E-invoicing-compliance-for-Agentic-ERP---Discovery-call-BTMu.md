## Topics

### E-Invoicing Partnership: MicroVista

  - [**Partner:** MicroVista, a proprietary e-invoicing platform and an approved ASP (Accredited Service Provider) by the UAE's FTA.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=123.0)
  - [**UAE Compliance Context:**](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=677.0)
      - [**Phased Rollout:** Mandate applies to companies with annual turnover \>50M AED.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=677.0)
      - [**Deadline:** Oct 31, 2026 (extended from July 31).](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=726.0)
      - [**Scope:** B2B and B2G invoices (sales and purchase).](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=634.0)
      - [**Process:** Seller → ASP (MicroVista) → PEPOL → FTA.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=461.0)
  - [**Partnership Model:** Agentic ERP will not become an ASP due to the 40-50k AED setup cost.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=1128.0)
      - [**Model 1 (Referral):** Introduce clients for a commission.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=1649.0)
      - [**Model 2 (Reseller):** Commit to a volume (e.g., 1M invoices/year for 100k AED) to secure a lower per-invoice rate (e.g., 10 fils).](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=1299.0)
  - [**Integration:** MicroVista provides APIs to handle the FTA's 52 mandatory invoice fields. The platform is hosted in the UAE cloud.](https://fathom.video/share/Nxw3TBVnAiQ8WuK7WL5yKvv5PAcPGBeJ?tab=summary&timestamp=1961.0)
