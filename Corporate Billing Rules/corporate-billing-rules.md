MPKAY  BILLING PROTOCOL FOR OPD AND IPD PATIENTS

CORPORATE                                             OPD                                IPD

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MPKAY <br>Emergency / Night Admission or Treatment : <br>If patient comes in the Emergency /Night or after closure of MPKAY offices then immediately :<br>1.  Register IPD<br>2. Admit patients and provide treatment.<br>3. Inform Billing Executive<br>4. Billing Executive will provide emergency certificates to patient relatives and intimate Medibuddy ONLINE with Claim ID generation.<br>5. If any urgent surgery or procedure is required, inform immediately to the Administrator or Billing Executive. They will immediately communicate to authorities concerned and update the Reception desk.<br>Important Note : Elective Procedures and Surgeries must be done with prior permission or intimation with proper documentation else hospital bill will not get sanction or may face deduction in the hospital bill | NO CASHLESS FACILITY<br>Patient need to pay OPD charges as per hospital tariffs <br>( Reception staff need to collect payment and provide proper Cash Receipt)<br>Note : Maharashtra Police provide Cashless treatment facility only for IPD Cases. | CASHLESS FACILITY<br>1. If the patientprovides a Police card, Adhar  card, Ration card, all required forms like MV,FD form... etc signed & stamped by the Police unit.<br>2. Case must beapproved by MPKAY Mumbai<br>(Note:  This information available with Billing Executive)<br>In case MPKAY deny or  reject Cashless treatment facility to admitted MPKAY patient <br>Then<br>admitted patients need to pay its hospital treatment bill as per hospital tariffs.<br>Undertaking : <br>At the time of admission  of a patient, the Billing Executive must take patient undertaking for  the payment of treatment  bill as per hospital tariffs for “Denied Cashless treatment “.<br>If MPKAY Denied Cashless treatment. |

CGHS  BILLING PROTOCOL FOR OPD AND IPD PATIENTS

        CORPORATE                             OPD                                        IPD

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CGHS <br>Hope Hospital is empanelled for Cardiology and Cardiac procedure and surgeries.<br>Emergency / Night Admission or Treatment : <br>If patient comes in the Emergency /Night or after closure of concern offices then immediately :<br>1.  Register IPD<br>2. Admit patients and provide treatment.<br>3. Inform Billing Executive<br>4. Billing Executive will provide emergency certificates to patient relatives and intimate  on CGHS ONLINE portal  with Claim ID generation.<br>5. If any urgent surgery or procedure is required, inform immediately to the Administrator or Billing Executive. They will immediately communicate to authorities concerned and update the Reception desk.<br>Important Note : Elective Procedures and Surgeries must be done with prior permission or intimation with proper documentation else hospital bill will not get sanction or may face deduction in the hospital bill. | NO CASHLESS FACILITY TO CGHS SERVING/WORKING EMPLOYEES<br>If CGHS serving/working employees come with OPD referral and CGHS ID Card then provide them concern treatment and give them “OPD treatment  Bill” as per CGHS 2014 Tariff. The patient will make the payment directly to the hospital.<br>The reception desk must  provide them with a Cash Receipt.<br>RETIRED CGHS BENEFICIARY/ EMPLOYEES WILL BE GIVEN CASHLESS FACILITY.<br>Patients will be given Cashless OPD treatment if the patient brings CGHS Signed and stamped Referral letter and CGHS Card copy.<br>Reception staff task : <br>1. Do OPD registration.<br>They will immediately inform the Billing Executive and  hand over these documents  to them. <br>2. Only Xerox/ True copies of Prescription, Investigation Reports etc. to be handed over to patients and all original copies of above documents to be handed over to Billing Executive.<br>Note : Billing Executive will do Online portal Intimation and Claim ID Generation and prepare  OPD billing as per CGHS 2014 tariff list and submit hard copies of bill along with all Investigation Reports to CGHS UTIITSL ( Shrimohini Complex) | CASHLESS FACILITY<br>If a patient provides CGHS signed & stamped Referral letters. CGHS Retired Employee/Beneficiary  Card. <br>Reception staff task :<br>Do IPD registration and admit patients.<br>Immediately inform the Billing Executive and  hand over these documents  to them.<br>Note for Billing Executives :<br>If patients demand facilities not entitled to them then take their concern undertaking. Hospitals need to collect payment of non entitled facilities as per CGHS tariff. If a facility is not listed in CGHS Tariff list then take payment as per hospital tariff.  <br>Note : Billing Executive will do Online portal Intimation and Claim ID Generation and prepare  IPD billing as per CGHS 2014 tariff list and submit hard copies of bill along with all Investigation Reports to CGHS UTIITSL ( Shrimohini Complex)<br>NO CASHLESS TREATMENT TO CGHS SERVING EMPLOYEES FOR IPD ADMISSION.<br>Reception Staff task : <br>Collect CGHS Referral and CGHS ID Card copies and hand over to Billing Executive.<br>At the time of admission  of patient Billing Executive must take patient undertaking for  the payment of treatment  bill as per CGHS 2014 TARIFF for listed procedure and for unlisted facilities payment as per hospital tariffs. |

CORPORATE                                               OPD                                IPD

|     |     |     |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ECHS<br>Not empanelled specialities:<br>Heart/Cardiac procedures/ Surgeries like CAG, CABG, Valve Replacement ...etc.<br>Emergency / Night Admission or Treatment : <br>If patient comes in the Emergency /Night or after closure of concern offices then immediately :<br>1.  Register IPD<br>2. Admit patients and provide treatment.<br>3. Inform Billing Executive<br>4. Billing Executive will provide emergency certificates to patient relatives and intimate  on ECHS ONLINE portal  with Claim ID generation.<br>5. If any urgent surgery or procedure is required, inform immediately to the Administrator or Billing Executive. They will immediately communicate to authorities concerned and update the Reception desk.<br>Important Note : Elective Procedures and Surgeries must be done with prior permission or intimation with proper documentation else hospital bill will not get sanction or may face deduction in the hospital bill. | CASHLESS FACILITY<br>Reception staff task: <br>Do OPD registration.<br>They will immediately inform Billing Executive and  hand over ECHS signed and stamped Referral letter and ECHS Card copy to Billing Executive.<br>Patients will be given Cashless OPD treatment if the patient brings ECHS signed and stamped Referral letter and ECHS Card copy handover it to Billing Executive.<br>Xerox/ True copies of Prescription, Investigation Reports etc to be handed over to patients and all original copies of above documents to be handed over to Billing Executive.<br>Note : Billing Executive will do Online portal Intimation and Claim ID Generation and prepare  OPD billing as per CGHS 2014 tariff list and submit ONLINE with endorsement of DSC. | CASHLESS FACILITY<br>Reception staff task: <br>If a patient provides ECHS signed and stamped IPD Referral letter and ECHS Card copy.<br>They will immediately inform the Billing Executive and handover these documents to the Billing Executive.<br>Note : Billing Executive will do Online portal Intimation and Claim ID Generation and prepare  IPD billing as per CGHS 2014 tariff list and submit ONLINE with endorsement of DSC.<br>Note : Undertaking for non  entitled  facilities :          <br>At the time of admission of ECHS patients, the Billing Executive must take patient undertaking for  the private payment for treatment/facilities asked or needed by patients but which are not entitled  to them. Provide patient not entitled healthcare facility (available with hospital) if undertaking is signed by patient and/or blood relative and collect payment as per hospital tariff for not entitled facility. |
