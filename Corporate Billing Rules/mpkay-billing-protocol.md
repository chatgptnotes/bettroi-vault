# MPKAY Billing Protocol - Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

### 1. Patient Admission & Documentation Collection

In Emergency admission of an MPKAY IPD patient, the following documents must be collected:

- Police ID Card (of the employee)
- MPKAY Card
- Ration Card
- Aadhaar Card (of both the employee and the patient)

Once all documents are collected, provide an Emergency Letter to the patient's relative.

* * *

### 2\. Immediate Forms & Documentation

After the patient's admission, the following forms must be completed immediately:

1. Form “A”

- This form must be filled and signed by the police employee from the Police Welfare Department.
- It contains two sections, both of which must be completed.

2. Family Declaration Form

- This form must be filled and signed by the police employee from their respective unit.

* * *

### 3\. Portal Registration & NOC Approval Process

Once the required documents are collected, proceed with the following steps:

1. Upload all collected documents on the IHX MPKAY portal including:

- Police ID
- Aadhaar Card
- Ration Card
- Member Verification (MV) and Family Declaration (FD)
- Admission Note
- Treatment Sheet
- Plan of Treatment (on hospital letterhead)

Check the IHX MPKAY portal for approval status:

- IR (Information Required): Address the query with the help of Murali Sir/RMO/Concerned Employee.
- Approval:

1. Inform the MPKAY patient and the concerned police employee.
2. Print the approval letter and attach it to the patient's IPD file.

- Denial:

1. Follow up with IHX MPKAY support to understand the reason and take corrective action. And counseling for further treatment for cash.

4.NOC Application Timeline:

| NOC Type | Duration | Application Process |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st NOC | 7 Days | Applied with a referral letter from the unit (welfare) on the IHX portal. |
| 2nd NOC | 14 Days | Applied through the IHX portal. |
| 3rd NOC | 21 Days | Applied through the IHX portal. |
| 4th NOC | More than 21 Days | Applied via email to DGP Office, Mumbai. |

### 5\. Patient Discharge Process

At the time of discharge, follow these steps:

1. Prepare the discharge summary in detail.
2. Obtain the patient's/police employee’s signature on every pharmacy bill (mandatory).
3. Provide Xerox copies of all investigations to the patient or the police employee concerned.

* * *

### 6\. IPD Billing & Claim Submission

1. The IPD bill file must be submitted within 7 working days to MPKAY Mumbai,4/B, Officers Quarters, Mumbai Police Commissioner Office Compound, Mumbai-400001- Mob:- 6366949625
2. The complete patient claim file must be uploaded on the IHX portal after billing submission.
3. The physical bill file must be sent via courier after online submission.
