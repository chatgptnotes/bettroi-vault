# tpa and insurance

Scrape ID: 019e39c2-fd5a-74c0-b07d-b11cb0cd6660
TPA’S & INSURANCE

Intimation Process

1) Collect all documents of patients i.e. Corporate Card, Policy Paper of Current Year, Aadhar Card, Identity Card. etc

2) Prepare the Admission Notes, Doctor Notes, Consultant Notes, treatment sheet from Doctor.

3) Fill the preauth form (Page no 2) according to treatment & filled by  Doctor & take the signature of the doctor, patient and their relative respectively.

Note : All company preauth form is stored in the folder which name is “PRE AUTH FORM”

Directory address :

\\\192.168.8.188\\HopeserverFromJuly2024\\ShashankFolder - TPA And Insurance\\PRE AUTH FORM

4) Scan all the documents like Corporate Card, Policy Paper of Current Year, Aadhar Card, pan card, EKYC Form (incase the treatment cost is 1.5 lac and above) Identity Card. Admission Notes, treatment sheet, preauth form, previous policy papers, previous investigation/radiology/ consultation notes or discharge summary. MLC (in case of accident or domestic fall) etc.

5) Send it to portal or email id.
