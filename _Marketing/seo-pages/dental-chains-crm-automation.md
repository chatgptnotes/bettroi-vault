# CRM Automation for Dental Chains


## Your CRM is a contact list. It should be a revenue engine.

Bettroi builds AI agents that run your dental chain's patient communication — recalls, follow-ups, treatment plan nudges — without your front desk having to touch a single template.

---

## The Problem

Multi-location dental chains generate thousands of patient touchpoints every month — post-extraction follow-ups, 6-month recall reminders, pending treatment plan closures — and almost all of it gets dropped because front desk staff are managing walk-ins and phones simultaneously. Your CRM has the data; nobody has the bandwidth to action it. The result: recall rates under 35%, pending treatment conversion in single digits, and zero visibility into which clinic is actually retaining patients.

---

## What We Build

- **Automated recall agent** — triggers WhatsApp + SMS sequences at 3-month, 6-month, and annual intervals, personalised per treatment history, with one-tap booking link
- **Pending treatment follow-up bot** — identifies patients with open treatment plans older than 14 days and runs a multi-step re-engagement sequence until they book or opt out
- **Post-visit care & review flow** — sends procedure-specific aftercare instructions within 2 hours of checkout, then requests a Google review at day 3 if sentiment is positive
- **Lead-to-first-appointment pipeline** — captures enquiries from WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and website forms into a single queue and auto-qualifies + schedules without receptionist intervention
- **Cross-clinic patient dashboard** — live view of recall due, pending treatments, and no-show rate per branch, piped into your existing CRM or a lightweight ops dashboard we wire for you
- **No-show recovery agent** — fires a rescheduling prompt within 30 minutes of a missed appointment with available slots pulled directly from your scheduling system

## How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

**Stage 1 — Discovery (2 weeks)**
We audit your current CRM setup, appointment data, and front-desk workflows. We map where patient communication is falling through the cracks and identify the 3 highest-ROI automation candidates specific to your chain — typically recall, pending treatment, and lead conversion.

**Stage 2 — Learn (30-day pilot)**
We deploy one agent — usually the recall bot, because the baseline data is cleanest — and run it live on one clinic. We measure recall booking rate before vs. after, response rate by channel, and front-desk time saved. You see real numbers, not a pitch deck.

**Stage 3 — Wire**
We integrate the winning agents into your actual systems — Dentally, Practo, Clinicpro, or whatever HMS and CRM stack you are running. No rip-and-replace. Agents sit on top of what you already have.

**Stage 4 — Automate**
Manual handoffs — receptionist forwarding recall lists, managers chasing no-shows — get replaced by agent orchestration. Your team handles exceptions; the agent handles volume.

**Stage 5 — Scale**
We roll the full stack across every branch with per-clinic performance monitoring, escalation rules, and a governance layer so your ops team stays in control as you add locations.

## Frequently asked questions

**We already have a CRM — Practo or Clinicpro. Do you replace it?**

No. We build agents that sit on top of your existing CRM and HMS. The agent reads appointment data, patient history, and treatment status from what you already have, then triggers communication through WhatsApp Business API, SMS, or email. You keep your current system; we make it actually do something.

**How long before we see results from the recall automation?**

In the Learn stage — 30 days on one pilot clinic — you will have a clear before/after on recall booking rate and front-desk time spent on outbound calls. Most dental chains we model see recall rates move from the 25-35% range to 50-60% within the pilot window. We measure it precisely so there is no guesswork going into the full rollout.

**What channels do the agents use — WhatsApp, SMS, email?**

We wire whichever channels your patients actually respond on. For Indian dental chains that is typically WhatsApp first, SMS as fallback, email for treatment plan documents. The channel logic is configurable per patient preference and per message type — a recall reminder and an aftercare instruction warrant different channels.

**Do we need a tech team on our side to manage this?**

No. The agents are built to run without your IT team intervening day-to-day. Your ops manager gets a dashboard and exception alerts. We handle the underlying infrastructure in our retained engagement, or we hand over documented runbooks if you want to self-manage after the Scale stage.

**Is patient data safe — DPDP Act, data residency?**

Yes. We architect all patient data flows to stay within India-resident infrastructure, use encrypted storage, and build with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act requirements in mind. We document the data flow explicitly in the Discovery audit so your compliance team can review before a single patient record touches the system.
