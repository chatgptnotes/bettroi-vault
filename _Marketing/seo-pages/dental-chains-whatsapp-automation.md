# WhatsApp Automation for Dental Chains

## Your front desk is spending 40% of its day on WhatsApp. Your patients still miss appointments.

Bettroi builds WhatsApp AI agents for dental chains that handle booking, reminders, post-treatment follow-ups, and feedback collection — automatically, across every branch, in the patient's preferred language.

---

## The Problem

Multi-branch dental chains run on appointment density, but front-desk staff manually chase confirmations, re-book no-shows, and answer the same 12 questions every single day on WhatsApp. At 3 branches this is annoying. At 10 branches it is a revenue leak — a missed chair slot in dentistry is ₹800–₹4,000 gone with no recovery. Meanwhile, patients expect instant replies at 9 PM when your receptionist is offline, and a delayed response often means they book with the competitor down the road.

---

## What We Build

- **Appointment booking agent** — patients send "book cleaning this Saturday" on WhatsApp and the agent checks live slot availability, confirms the branch, and writes the appointment into your HMS or Google Calendar without human touch
- **Reminder + confirmation bot** — automated 48-hour and 2-hour reminders with a one-tap confirm/reschedule flow, designed to cut no-show rates measurably
- **Post-treatment follow-up sequence** — day-1 and day-7 check-in messages after procedures like RCT or implant placement, flagging pain/concern replies to the clinical team instantly
- **FAQ deflection agent** — handles "what is the cost of braces", "do you accept insurance", "is parking available" without involving staff, with escalation to a human for anything outside scope
- **Feedback and review nudge** — automated Google Review or internal NPS prompt at the right moment post-visit, routed per branch so review scores are tracked by location
- **Reactivation campaign agent** — identifies patients who haven't visited in 6+ months and runs a WhatsApp re-engagement sequence with a personalised recall message

## How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We do not drop a chatbot and disappear. Every engagement runs through five structured stages so the automation actually sticks.

**1. Discovery (Week 1–2)**
We audit your current WhatsApp workflows across all branches — volume of inbound messages, average response time, drop-off points in the booking funnel, and which manual tasks consume the most front-desk hours. We identify the three highest-ROI automation candidates specific to your chain's operating model.

**2. Learn (Weeks 3–6)**
We pilot the top-priority agent — typically the booking + reminder bot — on one branch. We measure baseline no-show rate, front-desk message volume, and booking conversion before vs. after 30 days of the agent running live.

**3. Wire (Weeks 5–8)**
Winning agents get integrated into your core systems: your HMS (Dentally, Practo, or custom), your CRM if you have one, and your WhatsApp Business API account. We handle the Meta BSP setup and number registration if needed.

**4. Automate (Weeks 7–10)**
Manual handoffs get replaced with agent orchestration. The booking agent talks to the reminder agent. The follow-up agent flags to the clinical coordinator. No human needs to monitor routine message threads.

**5. Scale (Weeks 10–12+)**
We roll the system out across all branches with per-branch configuration (different doctors, different slot templates, different languages), plus a monitoring dashboard so your ops manager can see agent performance, escalation rates, and no-show trends in one place.

## Frequently asked questions

**We already use WhatsApp Business manually. Why do we need an AI agent?**

WhatsApp Business lets you send messages. An AI agent lets you handle conversations — booking, confirming, rescheduling, following up — without a human in the loop. At 3+ branches, the manual model does not scale. An agent replies in under 5 seconds at 11 PM; your front desk does not.

**Which WhatsApp Business API providers do you work with?**

We are BSP-agnostic. We have worked with Interakt, Wati, Gupshup, and direct Meta Cloud API setups. If you already have a BSP, we build on top of it. If you are starting fresh, we will recommend the right one based on your message volume and budget.

**Can the agent handle multiple languages — Hindi, Tamil, Telugu?**

Yes. The agents we build are multi-lingual at the conversation level. The patient writes in their language, the agent responds in kind. We configure the languages relevant to your branch locations during the Wire stage.

**What happens when the bot cannot answer something — does the patient get stuck?**

No. Every agent has a defined escalation path. If a message falls outside the agent's scope — a complex complaint, a medical question, a billing dispute — it routes immediately to a human with full conversation context. The agent never leaves a patient in a dead end.

**How do you integrate with our HMS? We use a custom-built system.**

We audit your HMS during Discovery. If it has an API, we wire to it directly. If it does not, we build a lightweight integration layer or use webhook-based workarounds. Custom HMS integrations are common in the dental space and we scope the complexity honestly before you commit to the Pilot.
