## What we build for hospitals

Bettroi designs, builds and deploys AI agents as working software — not slide decks. Typical artifacts we deliver:

- **OPD triage agent** — reads incoming patient history, flags high-priority cases and pre-fills the doctor's consultation form before the patient sits down

## How we do it: the Be AI-First Framework

We do not drop a chatbot and leave. Every engagement runs through five stages:

**1. Discovery (2 weeks)**
We audit your current workflows — OPD, IPD, billing, discharge, housekeeping coordination. We identify the 3 workflows with the highest ROI for AI intervention and size the effort honestly. You get a written report regardless of whether you proceed.

**2. Learn (weeks 3–8)**
We run one agent on one live workflow. We measure your baseline — time per task, error rate, staff hours — then run the agent for 30 days and show you the delta in writing.

**3. Wire (weeks 5–10)**
Winning agents get integrated into your HMS (Practo, Insta, eHospital, or custom), WhatsApp Business API, and any TPA portals you use. No rip-and-replace.

**4. Automate (weeks 8–12)**
Manual handoffs between departments get replaced with agent-to-agent orchestration. Admission → bed allocation → billing → discharge becomes a monitored pipeline, not a game of telephone.

**5. Scale**
We roll out across departments or sister hospitals with audit logs, governance rules, and staff training baked in. You own the agents — we stay on retainer only if you want us to.

## Frequently asked questions

**Will AI agents work with our existing HMS (Practo / Insta / eHospital)?**

Yes. We wire agents into your HMS via API or RPA layer depending on what your system exposes. We have worked with Practo, eHospital, and custom-built HMS setups. The Discovery audit confirms integration feasibility before you commit to a pilot.

**Do patient records or Aadhaar data get sent to an AI model?**

No. Our agent architecture includes a pre-flight allowlist that strips or flags personally identifiable data — including 12-digit Aadhaar numbers — before any prompt reaches an AI model. We also structure contracts so your hospital remains the data controller. If your institution requires a stricter on-premise setup, we scope that separately.

**How long before we see measurable results?**

The Learn stage runs for 30 days on one live workflow and produces a written baseline-vs-AI comparison. Most hospitals see time-per-task reductions of 40–70% on documentation and billing workflows. We measure and report it; you decide whether to proceed.

**We already have an IT team and a vendor for our HMS. How does Bettroi fit in?**

We work alongside your existing IT team and HMS vendor, not instead of them. Bettroi's scope is the AI agent layer — the logic that reads, decides and writes — not the HMS itself. We produce integration specs your IT team can review and approve before any code touches production.

**What happens after the engagement ends? Do we depend on Bettroi to keep the agents running?**

You own all agents and code delivered. We document everything so your IT team can maintain it independently. Retainer support is optional — typically hospitals use it for the first 3–6 months while staff get comfortable, then manage in-house or call us for new workflows.
