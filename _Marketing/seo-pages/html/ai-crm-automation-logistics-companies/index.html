The Problem

Logistics CRMs are graveyards of stale leads and missed follow-ups. Your business development team spends 2–3 hours a day updating deal stages, copy-pasting tracking numbers into emails, and manually logging calls — tasks that don't move a single consignment. Meanwhile, freight inquiries that come in after 6 PM sit unattended until morning, and a competitor quotes the same customer overnight. Add to this the fact that most logistics CRMs (Zoho, Salesforce, HubSpot) are configured out-of-the-box and never tuned to how a freight or 3PL business actually works — so adoption is low and the data is unreliable.

How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We don't hand you a generic automation template. Every engagement runs through five structured stages:

1. Discovery (Week 1–2) We audit your current CRM setup, lead sources, sales motion, and where your ops team loses time. We identify the 3 highest-ROI automation candidates specific to your freight or logistics business — typically inquiry handling, quote follow-up, and shipment comms.

2. Learn (Weeks 3–6) We deploy one AI agent on your live pipeline — usually the inquiry intake or follow-up bot — and run it in parallel with your current process for 30 days. We measure baseline response time, follow-up rate, and deal conversion before vs. after.

3. Wire (Weeks 5–8) Winning agents get integrated into your core stack — Zoho CRM, Salesforce, HubSpot, or a custom TMS — via API. No ripping and replacing your existing tools.

4. Automate (Weeks 7–10) Manual handoffs between sales, ops, and customer service get replaced with agent orchestration. A new freight inquiry triggers a chain: CRM record created → quote requested from pricing team → follow-up scheduled → customer notified on booking — zero human clicks for the routine path.

5. Scale (Weeks 10–12+) We roll out across branches, sales verticals, or geographies with monitoring dashboards and governance rules so your ops head can see exactly what the agents are doing and override when needed.

Frequently asked questions

We already have Zoho CRM set up. Will your agents work with it or replace it?

We work with your existing CRM — Zoho, Salesforce, HubSpot, or a custom TMS. Our agents connect via API and sit on top of your current setup. You keep the tool your team already knows; we just stop it requiring manual data entry and follow-up.

How long before we see results from the pilot?

The 30-day Learn phase gives you a direct before-vs-after comparison on the specific workflow we automate first — typically inquiry response time, follow-up rate, or quote conversion. Most clients see measurable improvement within the first 2 weeks of the pilot going live.

Our freight business has custom rate cards and non-standard shipment types. Can the agents handle that?

Yes. The inquiry intake and quote follow-up agents are configured to your actual commodity types, lane structures, and pricing logic — not a generic freight template. We spend the first two weeks of Discovery mapping exactly how your quoting and booking process works before writing a single automation.

What happens if an agent makes a mistake — sends the wrong update to a customer or misroutes a lead?

Every agent is built with a human-escalation path. Anything outside a defined confidence threshold gets flagged to a rep before it goes out. During the Scale stage we also set up a monitoring dashboard so your ops lead can see every agent action in real time and override or retrain as needed.

We have 3 branches in different cities. Can the automation work across all of them?

That is exactly what the Scale stage is designed for. After the pilot validates the model in one location, we parameterise it for each branch — different sales teams, different carrier panels, different response SLAs — and roll out with branch-level reporting so you can see performance per location.
