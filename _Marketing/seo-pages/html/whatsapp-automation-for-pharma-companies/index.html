The Problem

Pharma field teams run on WhatsApp already — but the conversations are unstructured, untracked, and generate zero usable data. Area managers chase 15–20 MRs daily just to collect DCRs (Daily Call Reports), and that data still has to be manually re-entered into the SFA. Stockists place indent orders over voice calls or informal chats, leading to fulfilment delays and stock-outs that nobody catches until the retailer complains.

What We Build

  • MR daily call report (DCR) bot — structured WhatsApp flow that captures doctor visits, samples distributed, and feedback in under 90 seconds; auto-syncs to your SFA
  • Stockist indent automation agent — stockists place reorders via WhatsApp; agent validates SKU, checks credit limit, and raises the order in your ERP without human intervention
  • Sample request and approval workflow — field reps request samples through WhatsApp; approvals route to the right manager and update inventory automatically
  • Adverse event and complaint triage bot — customer or retailer reports a product complaint on WhatsApp; agent logs, classifies by severity, and escalates to pharmacovigilance team within the same thread
  • Scheme and offer broadcast agent — push monthly trade schemes to 500+ stockists with personalised tier-based messaging; track reads and redemptions automatically
  • Compliance reminder engine — automated WhatsApp nudges for expiry tracking, cold-chain deviation alerts, and regulatory submission deadlines

How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We do not hand you a chatbot and disappear. Every engagement follows five stages:

1. Discovery (Week 1–2) We audit your current field-force workflow, stockist communication chain, and SFA/ERP stack. We identify the 3 WhatsApp automation use-cases with the highest measurable ROI — typically DCR collection, stockist ordering, and scheme broadcasts.

2. Learn (Weeks 3–8) We deploy a pilot agent on one workflow — usually the DCR bot — with one region or one product division. We measure baseline time-per-report and data accuracy vs. the automated agent over 30 days.

3. Wire (Weeks 5–10) Winning agents are integrated into your core systems — SFA (Salesforce, Nuvama, or custom), ERP (SAP, Tally, or otherwise), and WhatsApp Business API. No re-keying of data.

4. Automate (Weeks 8–11) Manual handoffs — area manager chasing MRs, customer service logging complaints, accounts reconciling stockist orders — are replaced with agent orchestration. Humans handle only exceptions.

5. Scale (Week 12 onwards) Agents roll out across all regions and product lines with monitoring dashboards, fallback handling, and governance so your compliance team can sleep at night.

Frequently asked questions

Do we need a WhatsApp Business API account before we start?

No. If you do not already have a WhatsApp Business API account, we help you get one set up with a BSP (Business Solution Provider) as part of the Discovery stage. Most pharma companies qualify within 3–5 business days. The API cost is separate from Bettroi's fees and is typically ₹0.35–₹0.70 per conversation depending on your BSP and volume.

Will this work with our existing SFA — Salesforce, Nuvama, or a custom system?

Yes, as long as your SFA has an API or a database we can connect to. During Discovery we assess your integration options. For legacy SFAs with no API, we have built lightweight middleware layers before. The Wire stage is specifically designed to connect agents to your existing stack without requiring you to replace anything.

Our MRs are not very tech-savvy. Will they actually use a WhatsApp bot?

WhatsApp is already the tool they use — we are just structuring the conversation. The DCR bot is designed as a simple back-and-forth: the agent asks questions, the MR replies in plain text or voice. In pilots we have seen adoption rates above 80% within the first two weeks because it is faster than filling a form.

How do you handle pharmacovigilance and regulatory compliance for complaint data collected over WhatsApp?

The adverse event triage bot classifies complaints by severity and routes them to your pharmacovigilance team with a full audit trail — timestamp, sender identity, original message, and agent classification. The data is stored in a structured log that can be exported for Schedule M or CDSCO reporting. We do not replace your PV process; we feed it cleaner, faster data.

What happens after the 12-week full transformation engagement?

You can move to our retained AI ops subscription (from ₹35,000/month), which covers agent monitoring, WhatsApp API uptime tracking, prompt updates when your workflows change, and addition of new use-cases. Alternatively, if your team wants to own the agents internally, we provide a 2-week handover with documentation and training.
