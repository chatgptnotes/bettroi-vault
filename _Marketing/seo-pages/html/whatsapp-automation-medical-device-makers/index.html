How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We don't drop a chatbot and disappear. Every engagement runs through five stages:

1. Discovery (Week 1–2) We audit your current WhatsApp usage, CRM/ERP data flows, and complaint-handling process. We identify the 3 highest-ROI automation candidates — typically dealer queries, complaint intake, and post-sale follow-ups.

2. Learn (Weeks 3–8) We pilot one agent on a live WhatsApp number. For 30 days we measure baseline (manual) vs. agent performance: response time, complaint capture rate, dealer satisfaction. You see exact numbers before committing further.

3. Wire (Weeks 6–10) Winning agents get integrated into your core systems — your ERP for order data, your QMS for complaint logs, your CRM for dealer records. We use WhatsApp Business API (Meta-approved) throughout.

4. Automate (Weeks 9–11) Manual handoffs — forwarding messages, updating sheets, creating tickets by hand — get replaced by agent orchestration. Your team handles only exceptions.

5. Scale (Week 12+) We roll out across product lines, geographies, or distributor tiers, with monitoring dashboards and governance rules so nothing slips through.

Frequently asked questions

Do we need the WhatsApp Business API, or can this work on our existing business number?

You need the official WhatsApp Business API (Meta-approved) for any automation at scale — bulk outbound messages, chatbot flows, and CRM integration all require it. We handle the API setup and BSP (Business Solution Provider) onboarding as part of the Discovery or Pilot engagement. If you already have a BSP, we connect to your existing account.

How does the complaint intake bot hold up under CDSCO MDR complaint-handling requirements?

The bot captures structured fields — device serial number, site, date of occurrence, nature of fault, reporter name — and writes them directly into your QMS or a dedicated complaints register with a timestamp and WhatsApp message ID for audit trail. It does not replace your MDR review process, but it eliminates the gap between a complaint being reported on WhatsApp and it being formally logged. We configure mandatory fields and escalation triggers per your SOP.

Our distributors are in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. Will they actually use a WhatsApp bot?

WhatsApp penetration in Tier 2 and Tier 3 India is near-universal among trade contacts. The dealer query agent works in plain conversational Hindi or English — no app download, no login, no training required. In our experience, adoption resistance is lower here than with any other digital channel because distributors are already on WhatsApp all day.

How long before the first agent is live?

If your WhatsApp Business API account is already active, the dealer query agent or complaint intake bot can be live in 3–4 weeks from the start of the Pilot engagement. API setup adds roughly 1–2 weeks. The 6-week Pilot timeline accounts for both.

Can the agents handle multi-language inputs — Hindi, Gujarati, regional languages?

Yes. The underlying LLMs handle Hindi and Hinglish well out of the box. Gujarati and other regional languages are supported with slightly more prompt engineering and testing. We benchmark language accuracy during the Learn stage and only wire the agent into production systems once accuracy meets your defined threshold.
