# HMS Modernization for Medical Device Makers Using AI Agents

## Your HMS was built for billing. Not for devices.

Bettroi builds AI agents that plug into your existing Hospital Management System and automate the device-tracking, sterilization-compliance, and implant-documentation workflows that your ops team is still doing by hand — cutting documentation overhead by 60%+ in the first pilot cycle.

---

## The Problem

Medical device makers selling into hospitals face a compounding compliance nightmare: your devices move through OT, CSSD, and ward inventory with minimal digital trail, sterilization cycle data lives in logbooks, and implant usage documentation (lot numbers, expiry, patient mapping) is reconciled manually after the fact — sometimes days later. HMS platforms like Ezovion, Insta HMS, or legacy HIS setups were not designed to capture device-level lifecycle data in real time. When a recall happens or an audit lands, your team is scrambling through paper.

---

## What We Build for You

- **Device lifecycle tracking agent** — captures serial number, sterilization cycle ID, and implant-to-patient mapping at point of use via structured HMS integration
- **Sterilization compliance bot** — pulls cycle logs from autoclaves or EO sterilizers and cross-checks against dispatch records, flagging exceptions before they become audit findings
- **Implant documentation agent** — auto-generates implant usage records (pacemakers, stents, ortho implants) with lot number, expiry, and surgeon sign-off, structured for CDSCO and hospital SOP compliance
- **Inventory reconciliation workflow** — daily agent-run reconciliation between HMS stock module and physical consignment inventory, with discrepancy alerts to the field rep
- **Recall readiness report** — on-demand agent that traces any device batch across all hospital sites where it was consumed, with patient and procedure context, in under 2 minutes
- **SOP knowledge agent** — grounds on your SOPs and device manuals (stored in a structured vault) so field staff and hospital OT coordinators can query device protocols in natural language

## How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We do not drop a chatbot and disappear. Every engagement runs through five stages:

**1. Discovery (Week 1–2)**
We audit your current HMS setup, device tracking workflows, and sterilization documentation chain. We identify the 3 highest-ROI AI candidates specific to your product lines — whether that's implants, single-use instruments, or capital equipment. Fixed-fee, no-obligation output.

**2. Learn (Weeks 3–8)**
We deploy one AI agent — typically the implant documentation agent or sterilization compliance bot — on a single hospital account. We measure baseline documentation time and error rate vs. the AI-assisted workflow over 30 days. Numbers on the table before we go further.

**3. Wire (Weeks 9–14)**
The winning agent gets integrated into your HMS (API, HL7 feed, or screen-scrape bridge, depending on what the HMS supports), your CRM for consignment tracking, and your internal SOP vault. No rip-and-replace.

**4. Automate (Weeks 15–18)**
Manual handoffs — field rep to depot, OT coordinator to biomedical, sterilization log to QA — get replaced with agent-orchestrated flows. Exceptions surface automatically. Your team handles exceptions, not routine data entry.

**5. Scale (Weeks 19–24+)**
We roll the system out across hospital accounts and geographies, with monitoring dashboards, governance rules, and a retained AI operations model if you want Bettroi running the agents on your behalf.

## Frequently asked questions

**Our hospital clients use three different HMS platforms. Can your agents work across all of them?**

Yes — we build HMS integrations case by case. We have worked with HL7-based integrations, REST APIs where the HMS exposes them, and structured screen-scrape bridges where APIs are not available. During the Discovery audit we map exactly what each HMS exposes and design the integration layer accordingly. You do not need your hospital clients to upgrade their HMS for this to work.

**We are already CDSCO-registered. Does HMS modernization affect our regulatory standing?**

The agents we build are documentation and tracking tools — they do not change your device classification or manufacturing process. For implant-class devices, the documentation agent actually strengthens your post-market surveillance trail by creating a machine-readable record of every implant event. We design all outputs to be exportable in formats your QA team can use for CDSCO submissions and internal audits.

**What happens if a hospital refuses to give us API access to their HMS?**

It is common. We have three fallback approaches: a lightweight data-entry agent your field rep uses at point of use (5-field form, 30 seconds), a WhatsApp-based capture bot that routes to your backend, or a scheduled data pull from whatever export format the HMS supports (Excel, CSV). The Discovery audit identifies which approach fits each hospital account.

**How long before we see measurable ROI from the pilot?**

The Learn stage runs for 30 days with baseline vs. AI metrics tracked from day one. In similar workflows — implant documentation and sterilization compliance logging — we typically see documentation time drop 50–70% and error-driven rework drop significantly within the first 30 days. We will show you the numbers from the pilot before asking you to commit to the full transformation.

**We have sensitive device and pricing data. How does Bettroi handle data security?**

All agents run on infrastructure you control or on isolated Bettroi tenants with no cross-client data sharing. SOPs and device manuals used as knowledge-base inputs are stored in a private vault (we use an Obsidian-backed architecture internally). We sign a standard NDA before the Discovery audit begins and can work within your data residency requirements, including India-only hosting.
