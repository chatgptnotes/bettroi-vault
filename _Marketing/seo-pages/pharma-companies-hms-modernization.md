**1. Discovery (Week 1–2)**

**2. Learn (Week 3–8)**
We deploy a pilot AI agent on the single highest-priority workflow. We measure your current baseline — time spent, error rate, headcount load — and compare it against AI performance over 30 days. You see numbers, not promises.

**3. Wire (Week 9–14)**
Winning agents get integrated into your live HMS, ERP, or LIMS environment. We handle API connections, data mapping, and access controls. No rip-and-replace — we work with your existing stack.

**4. Automate (Week 15–18)**
Manual handoffs between departments — QC to warehouse, procurement to finance, compliance to management — get replaced with agent orchestration. Approvals route automatically. Exceptions escalate with context attached.

**5. Scale (Week 19–24)**
We roll out across plants, depots, or business units with monitoring dashboards, access governance, and retraining protocols when your workflows evolve.
