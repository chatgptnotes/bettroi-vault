# CRM Automation for Power Plants

## Your CRM is a glorified spreadsheet that three people refuse to update

Power plant operations run on long-cycle relationships — EPC contractors, fuel suppliers, grid operators, state DISCOMs, OEM service partners. But the CRM that's supposed to track all of it gets updated only when someone remembers, or when a renewal is already overdue. Your commercial team is chasing the same DISCOM contact on WhatsApp because the last three follow-ups weren't logged. A fuel supply agreement lapsed 11 days before anyone noticed because the renewal alert sat in an inbox nobody monitors.

This is not a discipline problem. It is a workflow design problem. And it is exactly what AI agents fix.

## What we build

- **Vendor follow-up agent** — monitors open RFQs, AMC renewals, and fuel purchase orders; auto-drafts follow-up emails and logs responses back into your CRM without manual entry
- **DISCOM and offtake communication bot** — tracks scheduled generation reports, deviation settlement deadlines, and PPA milestone communications; flags overdue items to the right person every morning
- **Outage coordination tracker** — links planned/forced outage records to O&M contractor tickets and auto-updates CRM deal stages when milestones are hit or missed
- **Contract renewal pipeline agent** — surfaces expiring AMCs, PPAs, and fuel supply agreements 90/60/30 days out; assigns owner, drafts renewal initiation email, and moves the deal forward without a meeting
- **Meeting and site-visit logger** — converts call transcripts and field visit notes into structured CRM records, tags by asset, counterparty, and next action
- **Commercial dashboard feed** — pushes daily CRM health summaries (open deals, overdue follow-ups, upcoming renewals) to your ops WhatsApp group or Slack channel

## How we do it — the Be AI-First Framework

**Stage 1 — Discovery (2 weeks)**
We audit your current CRM setup (Salesforce, Zoho, HubSpot, or homegrown), interview your commercial and O&M teams, and map every manual handoff that touches external counterparties. We identify the 3 workflows leaking the most time and deal value.

**Stage 2 — Learn (weeks 3–8)**
We deploy one agent — typically the vendor follow-up or contract renewal agent — on live data. We measure baseline response times and follow-up completion rates, then compare after 30 days of AI-assisted operation.

**Stage 3 — Wire (weeks 5–10)**
Winning agents are integrated into your CRM via API or scheduled data sync, connected to your email, and optionally to your SCADA event feed for outage-triggered workflows.

**Stage 4 — Automate (weeks 8–12)**
Manual handoffs are replaced with agent orchestration. Deal stage updates, follow-up sequences, and renewal alerts run without anyone logging in to push them.

**Stage 5 — Scale**
We roll out across additional assets, plants, or regional offices with monitoring dashboards and governance controls so your team stays in the loop without doing the data entry.

## Frequently asked questions

**We already have Salesforce or Zoho. Do we need to replace it?**

No. We build agents on top of your existing CRM, not instead of it. The agents read from and write to whatever system you already use — via API, scheduled export, or email integration depending on what your IT team will allow. The CRM stays. The manual work goes.

**Our counterparties are DISCOMs and government entities. Can the agent handle formal communication norms?**

Yes. The communication agents are trained on your own templates and tone. They draft, they do not send autonomously — your commercial team reviews and approves before anything goes out to a DISCOM or regulatory body. You keep control of every external communication.

**How does the agent know when an outage or operational event happens?**

We can connect to your SCADA or EMS event feed if an API or scheduled export is available. Where it is not, we use a lightweight manual trigger or email parsing. Full SCADA integration is a Stage 3 (Wire) activity — the Stage 2 pilot can run on CRM data alone.

**What does the Discovery audit actually deliver?**

A written report with: a map of your current commercial and vendor workflows, time-and-cost estimates for the top 3 manual handoffs, a prioritised build recommendation with expected ROI range, and a 12-week delivery roadmap if you decide to proceed. It is actionable on its own even if you do not continue with us.

**How long before we see actual time savings?**

The Pilot stage runs for 30 days with baseline measurement from day one. Most power plant commercial teams see 40–60% reduction in follow-up preparation time within the first three weeks of the pilot, before we have even completed the full integration.
