PHASE N · PASSIVE CAPTURE
N
Slack Channel Watcher
The brain listens to every channel it's invited to. Decisions auto-captured.
👁️
What it does

Every message in every channel the bot is in gets scored 0–1 by Claude Haiku. Messages scoring 0.7+ are auto-ingested with type tag: decision · commitment · blocker · status. Bot adds 🧠 reaction so you know it captured.

Scoring guide
  • 1.0 = explicit decision ("we'll go with X")
  • 0.9 = commitment ("I'll send proposal Friday")
  • 0.8 = blocker ("can't proceed until X")
  • 0.7 = key status ("demo went well, signed off")
  • 0.5↓ = general discussion, questions, social — skipped
Channel #general, #random, #announcements, #lounge are automatically skipped (configurable). Cost: ~$0.10/day at 1000 messages — negligible.
