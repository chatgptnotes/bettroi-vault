# 🔭 Vision (1–3 years)

> The longer horizon behind the quarterly goals. The advisor uses this for "are we drifting"
> checks — work that doesn't ladder up to any of these is flagged.

## Hope Hospital
- Hope + Ayushman established as the region's **reference AI-first hospitals** — AI embedded across the full patient journey (registration → OPD/IPD → diagnostics → pharmacy → billing → discharge), running on data, not heroics.
- Grow the group from ~₹12 cr toward **₹30 cr+ annual run-rate** via occupancy, the Ayushman ramp, and selective specialty/site expansion.
- **NABH-accredited, quality-systemized** operations with measurable outcomes (occupancy, no-show, revenue leakage, length-of-stay) that prove the AI-first model works.

## Bettroi / HopeTech
- A **product + consulting firm**: AI-driven products for engineering, medical, and other businesses — recurring (SaaS) revenue, not just one-off projects.
- Known as the firm that takes a business **"from chatbot-curious to production AI"** — with Hope Hospital as the flagship proof case.
- A repeatable catalogue: the productized hospital AI-ops package + 2–3 vertical AI products, each with reference clients. Scale toward **₹5–10 cr+ run-rate**.

## Personal
- Shift from **operator to owner/architect** — spend time on strategy, product vision, partnerships, and dealmaking; delegate daily execution.
- A leadership team that runs **both the hospitals and Bettroi without you in the critical path**.
- A recognized voice in **AI-first healthcare** — the playbook, the Second Brain as a demonstrated system, and a seat at the table others want.
