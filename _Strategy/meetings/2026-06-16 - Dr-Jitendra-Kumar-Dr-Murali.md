# Dr Jitendra Kumar & Dr Murali

*2026-06-16 — [Open in Fathom](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg)*

## Meeting Purpose

[Explore a collaboration to bring AI solutions to Macula Healthcare's hospital clients.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=604.0)

## Key Takeaways

  - [**Collaboration Focus:** The partnership will center on Dr. Murali’s AI layer, not his full HIS. This is a strategic pivot, as Macula Healthcare sells intelligence and consulting, not software products.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1529.0)
  - [**Market Strategy:** Success requires a clear cost-benefit analysis (CBA) for each specialty. Hospitals have low EBITDA and need to see a direct ROI (e.g., staff reduction, cost savings) to justify AI investment.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=746.0)
  - [**Product Suite:** Dr. Murali’s company, Betroy, offers an AI layer ("second brain" + "agent factory") and a separate NABH documentation tool (`NABH.online`).](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=382.0)
  - [**Next Step:** A formal demo of the AI layer is required for Macula Healthcare to evaluate the technology and build a compelling sales narrative.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1970.0)

## Topics

### Dr. Murali's AI Solutions (Betroy)

  - [Dr. Murali, an orthopedic surgeon and hospital owner, co-founded Betroy in Dubai to help businesses adopt AI.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=382.0)
  - [**Core AI Platform:**](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=420.0)
      - [**"Second Brain":** A central data repository (using `.md` files) that provides context for AI agents to automate workflows.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1646.0)
      - [**"Agent Factory":** A tool for employees to map and automate their own daily tasks, enabling incremental, bottom-up automation.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1805.0)
      - [**Tech Stack:** Next.js, Node.js, Supabase, Vercel.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=664.0)
      - [**Cost:** \~₹2–3 lakhs for a 100-bed hospital, including source code. Recurring server costs are low (\~₹4–5k/month).](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=643.0)
  - [**NABH.online:** A separate application that generates \~4,000–5,000 hospital-specific documents (SOPs, evidences) for NABH accreditation.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=540.0)

### Macula Healthcare's Market Strategy

  - [Macula Healthcare is a 15-year hospital consulting firm specializing in setup, funding, design, and NABH accreditation.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=146.0)
  - [**Key Insight:** Hospital clients require a clear, specialty-specific CBA for AI adoption.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=746.0)
      - [**Rationale:** Hospitals operate on tight margins (low EBITDA) and need to see a direct ROI, such as staff reduction or cost savings.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=746.0)
  - [**Strategic Pivot:** Macula Healthcare will focus on selling the AI layer as an intelligence tool, not the full HIS.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1529.0)
      - [**Rationale:** This aligns with their consulting model of selling intelligence, not products, and avoids the challenge of replacing a client's existing HIS.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=1529.0)

### Proposed Collaboration

  - [Dr. Murali proposed a partnership where Betroy's AI services are integrated into Macula Healthcare's client offerings.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=604.0)
  - [**Example ROI:** An AI solution at RB Diagnostics (a diagnostic chain) automated handwritten report typing, enabling the reduction of 20 staff members. This concrete example of cost savings is the type of justification Macula Healthcare needs.](https://fathom.video/share/xxZYsvYesoj5kK3kp76-AhejH-z2LDmg?tab=summary&timestamp=907.0)
