# AI Workflow Automation for Logistics Companies

## Your ops team is drowning in manual handoffs that AI should already be handling.

Bettroi builds AI agents that automate the repetitive, error-prone workflows inside logistics operations — dispatch coordination, freight documentation, exceptions handling — so your people focus on decisions, not data entry.

---

## The Problem

Logistics operations run on dozens of micro-handoffs: booking confirmations, POD uploads, rate queries, exception escalations, invoice reconciliation. Each one is manual, each one introduces delay, and each one is a point of failure when volume spikes. Most logistics companies have the data to automate 60–70% of these handoffs but no internal team to build the agents. The gap between "we should automate this" and "this is automated" stays open for years.

---

## What We Build

- **Shipment exception agent** — monitors live shipment data, auto-escalates delays above threshold, drafts customer notification in your voice
- **Freight documentation bot** — auto-generates eBL drafts, commercial invoices, and packing lists from booking data; flags missing fields before submission
- **Rate desk automation** — ingests spot rate queries via email/WhatsApp, checks carrier rate cards, returns a formatted quote in under 2 minutes
- **POD reconciliation agent** — matches uploaded PODs against open deliveries, flags discrepancies, pushes matched records to your TMS or ERP
- **Vendor invoice validation bot** — cross-checks carrier invoices against contracted rates, auto-approves within tolerance, queues exceptions for human review
- **Daily ops summary agent** — pulls data from TMS, email, and WhatsApp threads; generates a structured morning ops brief for dispatchers and account managers

## How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We do not hand you a generic SaaS tool. We follow a 5-stage build process anchored in your actual workflows.

### Stage 1 — Discovery (Week 1–2)
We audit your current freight workflows: where handoffs happen, where data lives, where time is lost. Output: a ranked list of the 3 highest-ROI automation candidates with effort and impact estimates.

### Stage 2 — Learn (Week 3–8)
We deploy a pilot agent on your top-ranked workflow and run it in parallel with your existing process for 30 days. We measure baseline vs. agent performance on time, error rate, and cost per transaction. You see the numbers before committing further.

### Stage 3 — Wire (Week 9–12)
Winning agents get integrated into your core systems — TMS, ERP, WhatsApp Business API, email, carrier portals. No rip-and-replace. We wire AI into what you already use.

### Stage 4 — Automate
Manual handoffs get replaced with agent orchestration. Dispatchers stop copy-pasting. Account managers stop chasing PODs. Ops managers get dashboards instead of spreadsheets.

### Stage 5 — Scale
We roll out across lanes, branches, or subsidiaries with monitoring, alerting, and governance so your ops team can see exactly what the agents are doing and override when needed.

## Frequently asked questions

**We already have a TMS. Will your agents work with it, not around it?**

Yes. We wire agents into your existing TMS via API or RPA layer depending on what the system supports. We have integrated with systems that do not have clean APIs before — it is solvable in most cases. We confirm feasibility during the Discovery audit before you commit to the Pilot.

**How long before we see actual time savings?**

The Pilot stage runs for 30 days with your live data. By day 30 you have a before-and-after measurement on the specific workflow we automated — time per transaction, error rate, volume handled. Most pilots show 40–70% reduction in manual handling time on the targeted workflow.

**Our data is spread across email, WhatsApp, and the TMS. Is that a problem?**

That is the standard logistics data environment, not an exception. Our agents are built to ingest from email inboxes, WhatsApp Business API, portal scrapes, and structured TMS exports. The Discovery audit maps exactly where your data lives and what clean-up, if any, is needed before the agent can operate reliably.

**Do we need an in-house tech team to work with you?**

No. You need one operations person who knows the workflow well enough to validate that the agent is doing the right thing. We handle architecture, build, integration, and deployment. Post-launch, we run a handover and offer retained ops if you want ongoing support without hiring.

**What if the pilot agent does not perform well enough to justify the next stage?**

Then we stop. The Pilot is structured specifically so you have a go/no-go decision point with real data before any further spend. If the agent does not hit a performance bar we agree upfront, we will tell you honestly and refund is not required because the measurement itself has value — you now know exactly why automation did not work and what needs to change.
