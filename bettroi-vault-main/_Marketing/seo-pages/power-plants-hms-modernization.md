# HMS Modernization for Power Plants Using AI Agents


## AI agents that turn your plant's health management system from a recordkeeping burden into an operational nerve centre.

Power plants run 24×7 on assets worth hundreds of crores. Your HMS should be doing the same. Most aren't.

## The Problem

Plant health management in most thermal, hydro, and renewable facilities still runs on a combination of Excel shift logs, paper-based permit-to-work registers, and a CMMS that nobody trusts because the data is 48 hours stale by the time anyone reads it. Occupational health records for 300+ contract workers are scattered across three vendor portals, two email threads, and a filing cabinet near the medical room. When a statutory audit lands — DGFASLI, OISD, or a client ESG audit — your safety team spends two weeks pulling data that should take two hours.

## What We Build

Bettroi designs and delivers specific AI agent systems for power plant HMS modernisation. Typical artifacts:

- **Occupational Health Intake Agent** — auto-ingests contractor medical fitness certificates, flags expiries 30 days out, and blocks permit-to-work issuance for non-compliant workers
- **Shift Health Log Digitisation Bot** — converts handwritten or voice shift-end logs into structured HMS records via OCR + LLM, eliminating 2–3 hours of daily data-entry per shift supervisor
- **Incident-to-Root-Cause Agent** — links near-miss and incident reports to equipment tags, contractor IDs, and work-permit history automatically; generates a draft CAPA within 24 hours
- **Statutory Compliance Calendar Agent** — tracks DGFASLI, OISD-GDN-192, and factory licence renewal deadlines; sends escalation alerts to the right role, not just a generic inbox
- **HMS–SCADA Bridge** — wires health and fatigue-risk signals from HMS into your SCADA/DCS dashboard so control-room operators see crew wellness alongside plant vitals
- **Audit-Pack Generator** — on demand, assembles a formatted compliance dossier (medical records, incident log, training register, permit history) in under 15 minutes

## How We Do It: The Be AI-First Framework

We do not hand you a product and disappear. We run a structured 5-stage engagement.

### Stage 1 — Discovery (2 weeks)
We audit your current HMS workflows end-to-end: permit-to-work, medical fitness tracking, incident reporting, statutory filings. We identify the 3 workflows where AI will deliver the fastest, most measurable ROI — typically the ones where your safety team is spending the most manual hours or where compliance gaps are highest.

### Stage 2 — Learn (weeks 3–6)
We deploy a pilot agent on one workflow — usually the Occupational Health Intake Agent or the Incident-to-Root-Cause Agent, because the baseline is easy to measure. We run it for 30 days against your actual data and show you the before/after: hours saved, error rate, compliance coverage.

### Stage 3 — Wire (weeks 7–9)
Winning agents get integrated into your core systems: your existing CMMS (SAP PM, IBM Maximo, or homegrown), your contractor management portal, and where relevant, your SCADA/DCS layer. We use OData services, scheduled exports, or direct API connectors depending on what your IT team will actually allow.

### Stage 4 — Automate (weeks 10–11)
Manual handoffs between HMS, HR, safety, and operations get replaced with agent orchestration. A contractor's medical certificate expiry automatically triggers a permit block — no human in the loop required.

### Stage 5 — Scale (week 12 onwards)
We roll out across all units and shifts, add monitoring dashboards, and set up governance protocols so your plant leadership has full visibility into what the agents are doing and why.

## Frequently asked questions

**Our plant already has a CMMS (SAP PM / Maximo). Do we need to replace it to work with Bettroi?**

No. We build AI agents that sit on top of your existing CMMS and pull data from it — via OData services, scheduled exports, or direct API where your IT team permits. The CMMS stays in place. The agents handle the intelligence layer: flagging, classifying, escalating, and generating documents. You are not buying a new platform; you are making your current one actually useful.

**What does 'HMS modernization' actually mean for a power plant? Is this the same as a hospital HMS?**

In the power sector context, HMS covers occupational health management, contractor medical fitness tracking, industrial hygiene monitoring, incident and near-miss reporting, and statutory compliance (DGFASLI, OISD, factory act filings). It overlaps with your safety management system. It is not the same as a hospital information system, though the data structuring principles are similar. Bettroi builds AI agents specific to the industrial health and safety record workflows your plant actually runs.

**How long before we see a measurable result?**

The Learn stage runs for 30 days on a single workflow and produces a quantified before/after comparison — hours saved, compliance coverage percentage, error rate. Most plants see a clear ROI signal within 6 weeks of the pilot start. The full 5-stage transformation takes 12 weeks from Discovery to Scale.

**Our plant has 400+ contract workers from multiple vendors. Can the system handle that complexity?**

Yes, and that is precisely the scenario where the Occupational Health Intake Agent earns its cost fastest. Multi-vendor contractor populations with rotating rosters, multiple certificate types, and different validity periods are exactly the kind of high-volume, high-variability data problem that LLM-backed agents handle well and that Excel or manual processes handle badly. The agent can ingest certificates from email attachments, vendor portals, or scanned PDFs and cross-reference them against your active permit-to-work roster in real time.

**We are a public sector utility. Can Bettroi work within our procurement and data-security constraints?**

We have structured engagements to work within fixed-fee procurement frameworks (the Discovery and Pilot packages are exactly that). On data security, all agent deployments can be configured for on-premise or private-cloud operation — your health and safety data does not need to leave your network. We are happy to provide a data-handling agreement and architecture diagram before you commit to anything.
