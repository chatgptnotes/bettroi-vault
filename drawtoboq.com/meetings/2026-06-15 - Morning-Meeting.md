### BOQ Discrepancies & Root Cause

  - [**LV Panel to SMDB Cables:** Lengths are approximately correct, with a standard \~5m per floor added for vertical rise.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=120.0)
  - [**SMDB to DB Cables:** Quantities are significantly different from expected.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=228.0)
      - [**Expected:** Calculate for one typical floor (e.g., 7th), then multiply by the number of floors (e.g., 8 or 16).](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=228.0)
      - [**Actual:** This efficient method was not used, causing the large discrepancy.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=228.0)
  - [**Lighting Fittings:**](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=398.0)
      - [**Expected:** Use only project-specific reference types from the drawings.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=398.0)
      - [**Actual:** The BOQ uses unclear descriptions, suggesting references from another project (possibly India-based) were mixed in.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=197.0)
  - [**Root Cause:** The BOQ was likely copied from another project instead of being calculated from the current drawings.](https://fathom.video/share/tEKQRuxNx79K8rY2yGtuD6YXxapq_gRR?tab=summary&timestamp=456.0)
