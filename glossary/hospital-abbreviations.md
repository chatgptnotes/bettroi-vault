# Hospital abbreviations

Heavily wikilinked. Add new terms here so other docs can `[[link to them]]`.

## Departments

- **OPD** — Outpatient Department.
- **IPD** — Inpatient Department (admitted patients).
- **ICU** — Intensive Care Unit.
- **OT** — Operation Theatre.
- **OR** — Operating Room (synonym for OT).

## Pharmacy / regulatory

- **MOQ** — Minimum Order Quantity. Some suppliers won't accept below this.
- **GST** — Goods and Services Tax. Indian indirect tax; medicines have category-specific rates.
- **Schedule G / H / H1 / X** — Indian regulatory classes for drug dispensing.
  - **G:** general sale, dispense with caution.
  - **H:** prescription-only.
  - **H1:** narcotic / habit-forming, prescription + dispensing register required.
  - **X:** highly controlled, narcotic-strength.
- **ESIC** — Employees' State Insurance Corporation. Government insurance scheme. Affects billing flow, not clinical care.

## Clinical

- **MRN** — Medical Record Number. Patient identifier.
- **ABHA** — Ayushman Bharat Health Account. National health ID, 14 digits.
- **DOB** — Date of Birth.
- **BP** — Blood Pressure (systolic/diastolic in mmHg).
- **HR** — Heart Rate (bpm).
- **SpO2** — Peripheral oxygen saturation (%).
- **CBC** — Complete Blood Count (lab panel).
- **ECG / EKG** — Electrocardiogram.
- **MRI** — Magnetic Resonance Imaging.
- **CT** — Computed Tomography.

## Workflow

- **DPDP** — Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. India's main data protection law.
- **DPO** — Data Protection Officer (organisational role under DPDP).
- **PHI** — Protected Health Information. The kind of data that must NOT leave the system without de-identification.
- **PII** — Personally Identifiable Information.
- **PO** — Purchase Order.
- **RLS** — Row-Level Security (Postgres / Supabase).
- **SOP** — Standard Operating Procedure.
- **TBC** — To Be Confirmed. Used as a placeholder; never published externally.
