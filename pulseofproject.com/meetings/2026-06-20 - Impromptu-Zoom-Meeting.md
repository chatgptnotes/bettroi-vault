### Maudi Portal (Ventri Portal)

  - [**Goal:** Automate the spare parts quotation process within a 6-week MVP timeline.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1603.0)
  - [**Workflow:**](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1231.0)
    1.  [**Lead Capture:** AI parses incoming email RFQs.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1231.0)
    2.  [**KYC:** Salesperson manually verifies the lead's legitimacy.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1298.0)
    3.  [**BOQ Generation:** AI creates a Bill of Quantities (BOQ) from the RFQ.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1350.0)
    4.  [**Approval:** The BOQ is sent to a regional Approver for human verification and stock confirmation with the Super Admin.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1380.0)
    5.  [**Client Response:** The approved BOQ returns to the Salesperson, who uses an AI-generated draft email to send the final quote.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1482.0)
  - [**Scope Expansion:** The MVP must now include a full CRM to manage leads and contacts from various sources (email, phone, tender portals).](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1618.0)
  - [**Action:** The team must study the client's detailed flowcharts to clarify all human and AI steps before the Monday meeting.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2153.0)

### Printo POC (AI Drawing to ERP Data Extraction)

  - [**Goal:** Build an AI layer on top of Printo's existing RealSoft ERP to automate data extraction from construction drawings.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2732.0)
  - [**Business Model:** This project demonstrates a key future strategy: building intelligent layers on top of existing client applications.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2810.0)
  - [**Workflow:**](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=1231.0)
    1.  [**Input:** Client uploads approved construction drawings.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2975.0)
    2.  [**Extraction:** AI extracts key data fields (e.g., drawing number, dimensions, materials).](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2975.0)
    3.  [**Validation:** The system validates extracted data against defined business rules.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=2955.0)
    4.  [**Output:** Verified data is pushed into the RealSoft ERP via an API provided by Printo.](https://fathom.video/share/ZctTnZkX72sWqNHxm2jQisDeuzCxfxW3?tab=summary&timestamp=3000.0)
